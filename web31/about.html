About Bangalore

Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital of the Indian state of Karnataka and the country's third most populated city. It is located in the southeast of the state on the Deccan Plateau, at an elevation of 3,113 feet (949 meters) above sea level. Bangalore is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" and is the country's leading IT employer and exporter.
